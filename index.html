Biography

Justin Bieber is a Canadian singer and songwriter. Discovered at a young age through his YouTube videos, he quickly rose to fame with his debut single "One Time". Over the years, Justin has evolved his music style, amassing a global fanbase and becoming one of the world's best-selling music artists. With numerous awards and accolades, Justin continues to influence the music industry with his innovative sounds and heartfelt lyrics.
